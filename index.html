Empowering Accessibility


A hands-free computer interface that uses eye tracking, voice commands, and AI to empower people with physical disabilities to fully control a computer—no mouse or keyboard needed.

About Aivue

Aivue leverages the power of Gemini and Openmind AI to enable hands-free computing for individuals with physical disabilities.

Key Features

  • Voice Command Integration:
    Use your voice to task the machine to do anything you need it to.
  • Head Movement Tracking:
    Utilize your head movements to command your cursor.
  • AI-Powered Assistance:
    Our AI Computer Use Agent will allow you to speak freely to yout computer without worrying about misinputs.
  • Seamless Accessibility Support:
    Aivue ensures effortless digital navigation through intuitive voice commands and responsivehead-tracking.
