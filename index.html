Empowering Accessibility


A hands-free computer interface that uses eye tracking, voice commands, and AI to empower people with physical disabilities to fully control a computer—no mouse or keyboard needed.

About Aivue

AIVue leverages the power of Gemini and machine learning technology to enable hands-free computing for individuals with physical disabilities.

Key Features

  • Voice Command Integration:
    Use your voice to type anything you need it to.
  • Head Movement Tracking:
    Utilize your head movements to navigate your computer.
  • AI-Powered CUA Agent:
    Our Computer Use Agent will allow you to speak freely to your computer to command it to do anything you need it to.
  • Seamless Accessibility Support:
    AIVue ensures effortless digital navigation through intuitive voice commands and responsivehead-tracking.
